# Lab Exercises

These structured lab exercises are designed for graduate coursework. Each lab builds on concepts from the corresponding book chapters and provides hands-on experience with r-sim.

| Lab | Topic | Prerequisite Chapters | Estimated Duration |
|-----|-------|----------------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Basic Simulation Setup | Ch 1–2 | 2 hours |
| 2 | Building a Custom Controller | Ch 3 | 3 hours |
| 3 | Multi-Task Dependencies | Ch 4 | 2 hours |
| 4 | FMU Integration | Ch 6 | 3 hours |
| 5 | I/O and Hardware Interfacing | Ch 5 | 3 hours |
| 6 | Full System Co-Simulation | Ch 5–8 | 4 hours |

Each lab includes:
- **Objective** — What you will accomplish
- **Background** — Key concepts to review
- **Steps** — Detailed instructions
- **Deliverables** — What to submit
- **Challenge** — Optional extension for advanced students
